少年你渴望力量吗{doge}

2022-08-30 #摘录

来源：21世纪英语报采编自 https://tinybuddha.com –Where does our strength come from?

抄者按：moran小朋友通常对高中鸡汤文不屑一顾，但是这篇写出味道了，闷一口真香

  1. Strength is a personal measurement for a truly unique, subjective experience.
  2. Strength comes from doing hard things: showing up despite the pain or fear, going through the struggle and then building on that to keep going forward and upward.
  3. We often go through things without realizing the massive effort it took to do something.
  4. Strength comes from tracking progress and celebrating it one tiny bit at a time.
  5. As you begin to discover and unmask more of yourself, you get to make choices that honor more of you, and you get to live your purpose. When we know better, we do better.
